R - Restart Game

Left - A
Right - D
Down - S
Up/Jump - W or Space

Sword - K or Left Mouse Button (press 3 times for combo)
Guns - J or Right Mouse Button
Dash - L or Shift

Lunging Stab - Down + Sword (can be canceled into from combo)
Dual Shoot - Down + Guns

Attack enemies with sword to refill ammunition
